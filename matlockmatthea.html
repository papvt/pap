Matthea Matlock



Project Name: Reach

Once one reaches their goal, or reaches the summit of a mountain, they must use the skills they learned to reach the next goal and to climb the next mountain. Each painting represents the next goal someone has after the one they have just achieved. The paintings become more and more abstract, to represent how a person can be unsure of their next goal, and how to achieve it. The abstractivity represents a person’s fear of the unknown once they have accomplished their nearest goal. As the path forward in a person's life becomes more uncertain, the painting also lets go of clarity, representing a reliance on feeling rather than form.
