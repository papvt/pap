Alexis Washington



Project Name: The Times it's not about US

As everything wrong with a white, male-dominated society, queer women of color bear the burden of understanding and the responsibility to fight. We imagine these women as martyrs and saviors but we forget that their lives are far beyond our history books. Somedays, some moments they were just people, struggling as people do. My multimedia sculpture honors these women by allowing viewers to peek into the excellence of Ball Culture and see the heart that goes into being a community mother.
