Savannah Peters



Project Name: PAP Apparel

The PAP merchandising team developed a clothing brand that translates Los Angelos culture, landmarks, and community identity into streetwear-inspired designs anchored by a unifying logo and runner motif. Product curation and design focused on representing small business inclusion and Olympic influence while exploring a mix of streetwear and high-fashion elements. The brand's direction was guided by the core values of power, access, and permanence.
