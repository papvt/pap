Project Name: PAP – Brand Merchandising (Permanence)

My group's project centered on building PAP as a clothing brand, with a focus on merchandising and bringing the brand identity to life through product curation. My specific contribution was researching and selecting the right pieces of clothing for the brand, identifying the styles, fits, and details that best represent what PAP stands for and how we can incorporate small business inclusion and the culture of LA around the Olympics.
